Cool Paintings

> A website that shows a few paintings I find interesting.

"Nighthawks" by Edward Hopper.
I like this painting because of the scene's atmosphere and the contrasted lighting.
The figures seem to be socializing, all but a man who's back is to the viewer.
The woman is named and modeled after Hopper's wife, Josephine.
It also makes for a cool background if you don't use desktop shortcuts.

"Triple-Self Portrait" by Norman Rockwell.
I like this painting because of the simplicity yet depth of the scene.
Rockwell is doing a simple self-portrait using a mirror, he's surrounded by the clutter of his workspace.
Most objects around him are references to other works of art.
The painting is an impressive display of skill in itself.
A painting of a man painting himself...
That's like painting squared.

"Garden of Earthly Delights" by Hieronymus Bosch.
I like this painting because of the wide, spanning landscape yet the abundance of small details found within.
Religious symbolism is heavily present throught the entirety of the work.
It's divided into 3 sections from left to right, dubbed "Garden of Eden, Heaven, Hell".
The more weirder and intersting parts are found in the "Hell" section.
